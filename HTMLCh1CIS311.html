Pancakes

Ingredients:

2 cups (241g) King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour

2 tablespoons (25g) sugar

1 tablespoon baking powder

3/4 teaspoon salt

2 large eggs

1 1/2 cups (340g) milk

1/4 cup (57g) melted butter or vegetable oil

Instructions:

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

In another bowl or large measuring cup, whisk together the eggs and milk.

Pour the liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir just until combined.

Stir in the melted butter or oil.
